ROSALES NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-SHS

Core Courses

The HUMSS strand includes the following core courses:

  • Introduction to Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Philosophy
  • Community and Service
  • General Psychology

Elective Subjects

Students can choose elective subjects based on their interests. Some examples include:

  • World History
  • Introduction to Political Science
  • Media and Information Literacy

Additional Courses

In addition to core courses and elective subjects, students may take extra courses such as:

  • Research and Writing
  • Applied Economics
  • Foreign Language Studies
