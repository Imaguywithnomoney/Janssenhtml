Welcome to the Agent-Based Modeling (ABM) subject! This subject provides a deep dive into the principles and applications of ABM, exploring the simulation of complex systems through individual agent interactions.

Subject Overview

Agent-Based Modeling involves creating autonomous agents with specific behaviors, rules, and interactions. Through simulations, students gain insights into emergent phenomena and understand the dynamics of complex systems in various fields.

Key Courses

  • Introduction to Agent-Based Modeling: This course covers the fundamental concepts of ABM, including agent behavior, rules, and the design of simulation environments.
  • Advanced Topics in ABM: Explore advanced techniques and applications of ABM, including real-world case studies and cutting-edge research in the field.
  • Practical Implementation of ABM: Hands-on course where students apply ABM concepts to create simulations, analyze results, and solve complex problems.

Career Opportunities

Graduates with expertise in Agent-Based Modeling find exciting career opportunities in research institutions, data analysis, policy modeling, and more. The skills acquired in these courses open doors to understanding and solving real-world challenges.
